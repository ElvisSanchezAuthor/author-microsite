The Deliberate, Perfect, Eternal (DPE) Framework

Elvis Sanchez is a metaphysical theorist whose work provides a rigorous, deductive challenge to established cosmological and theological views. Holding a master’s degree in education, Sanchez developed the groundbreaking **DPE framework**—an architectural model that defines **Time as a corrosive rupture**, rather than a feature of creation. He rejects metaphorical speculation in favor of logical necessity, offering a clear, coherent system for the **Intuitively Bothered** reader.
